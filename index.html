Experience Life in the 1800s.

Hands on History Utah invites families and groups to step into a living story of the American West.

Experience Life in the 1800sHistorical AdventurePlan Your Visit
Hands on History Utah is an interactive historical adventure where families and groups experience life in the 1800s through hands-on experiences, teamwork, and storytelling.

Families

Explore at your own pace. Choose where to go, what to try, and how your family wants to experience the story.

Groups

Schools, scouts, churches, youth groups, and other organized groups can schedule the signature Historical Adventure.

Supporters

Help preserve history and create hands-on experiences for future generations through donations, volunteering, and sponsorships.

History you can touch.

Pan for gold, make adobe bricks, send a telegraph message, experience the Pony Express, visit a Native American village, and discover how people worked, traveled, communicated, and survived in the 1800s.

Coming soon

Our first website version is the foundation. A research section, interactive map, QR-code experiences, and deeper historical pages can be added as the project grows.
