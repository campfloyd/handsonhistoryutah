Signature Group Program

Historical Adventure

Designed for schools, scout troops, church groups, youth organizations, family reunions, and other organized groups.

A shared journey through history.

The Historical Adventure is our signature group experience. Teams move through one connected story where history comes alive through teamwork, leadership, problem solving, and hands-on experiences.

It is more than a tour and more than a game. It uses game mechanics, rotating leadership, and storytelling to help participants experience the 1800s together.

Schedule a Group Visit

Who is it for?

Schools, scouts, church groups, youth groups, community organizations, family reunions, and leadership groups.


What does it teach?

History, teamwork, communication, leadership, decision making, and problem solving.

How it works

Teams travel together

Groups move through the experience as teams, with each part of the adventure connected to the larger story.

Leadership rotates

Participants get opportunities to lead, listen, communicate, and help the team succeed.

History becomes active

Hands-on experiences turn history into something participants can feel, remember, and talk about.
