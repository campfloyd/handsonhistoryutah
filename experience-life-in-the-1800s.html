Family Experience

Experience Life in the 1800s

Families can explore the story in any order. The map and directions will help visitors choose their own path through hands-on experiences inspired by life in the American West.

Explore the map

This map area is ready for the final site map. Each location will show what visitors can expect and the story behind the experience.

What to expect

Gold Panning

Experience the excitement of searching for gold as prospectors once did in the American West.

Adobe Brick Making

Mix mud, sand, and straw, then form an adobe brick using traditional building methods.

Pony Express

Experience the speed, responsibility, and urgency of carrying messages across the frontier.

Telegraph

Send messages with clicks and signals while discovering how communication changed in the 1800s.

Native American Village

Learn about the first people of the region and the ways they lived, adapted, and built community.

River Crossing

Work together to understand one of the most difficult obstacles faced by travelers moving west.
