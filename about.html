About Hands on History Utah

Hands on History Utah exists to help people experience the past, not just read about it.

Mission

Hands on History Utah is an interactive historical adventure where families and groups experience life in the 1800s through hands-on experiences, teamwork, and storytelling.

Vision

Our vision is to preserve the history of the American West by inspiring curiosity, strengthening families and communities, and creating unforgettable experiences where people don't just learn history—they live it.

Hands on History Utah is connected to Friends of Camp Floyd and the effort to preserve, teach, and share the history of Fairfield, Camp Floyd, Cedar Valley, and the American West.
